Signs Your Cape Town Home Needs Repainting ‚Äì 4 Warning Signs

As professional painting contractors in Cape Town, we identify these common issues that require immediate attention

FADED PAINT

Is generally a sign that the existing paint has aged substantially and in many cases the surface becomes "CHALKY" when rubbing your hand across the surface. To avoid further deterioration which becomes costly, contact a professional painting contractor such as GM Painting Contractors for advice.

CRACKS

Are generally due to settlement that takes place in plaster work, movement cracks and or structural cracks. Failing to repair will create a source for penetrating damp, fungi growth and possible cause for delamination of plaster work in certain areas. This also can be in the long term costly if not addressed at the early stages.

PAINT PEELING & FLAKING

Is normally caused by bubbles / blisters which break open and then expose the underlying surface which will become a source of moisture penetration which once getting behind the paint will cause further deterioration and once again on long term will become costly.

BLOTCHING / DISCOLOURATION OF PAINT

This is an indication that the surfaces affected have damp in the brick and plaster work underlying which is having a chemical reaction with the colour pigment which in turn is burning the colour pigment causing the white-ish spots. Ignoring this will eventually become unsightly and affect the aesthetics of the exterior of the building.
